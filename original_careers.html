This is the complete tier breakdown for the 125 trade categories we listed earlier, written out (no numbering). Open this file in a browser to view.

Tier meanings (Colorado/Denver-oriented):
  • T0: no multi-year license ladder (business setup + insurance; local registration may apply)
  • T1: short certifications/registrations (months; not apprenticeship)
  • T2: journeyman-equivalent ladder (~4 years) for the work class
  • T3: master-level (journeyman + additional time) when you personally qualify permits as the business
  • T4: supervisor/qualifier ladder (common in mechanical/refrigeration/gas/boiler/steam scopes; often 4/6/8-year tracks)
  • T5: you can own/sell/manage immediately, but permitted scopes are handled by a licensed qualifier/sub unless you later climb T2ΓÇôT4
